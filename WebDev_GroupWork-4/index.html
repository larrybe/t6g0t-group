What to know before you go.

The most immersive Game of Thrones experience yet.

Planned and built by the people who brought you the most famous TV series of all time, Westeros Northern Ireland places you in the fantastical and thrilling world of Game of Thrones. Built within minutes of many of the filming locations used in the production of the TV series, Westeros Northern Ireland will feel familiar to all fans of the show.

Make your reservation

Both a valid ticket and a park reservation is required to enter the theme park. Schedule your park visit with the park reservation system. Park reservation availablility is limited, not guaranteed and subject to change.

Experiences and Attractions

The WildFireWorks Show

Every Saturday night. Experience being at the Battle of the Blackwater with the most spectacular and innovative fireworks show on earth.

Tyrion's Axe Throw

Learn to weild the weapon of choice for Tyrion Lannister.

Purple Wedding Reinactment

Be a guest at the second most memorable wedding in TV history.

Archery at Winterfell

Have an Archery lesson from Winterfell's master-at-arms.

Dark Hedges Tunnel

Walk from the Red Keep to Winterfell through this scenic tunnel.

Ride The Drogon™

The Drogon™ is a roller coaster designed and engineered to minic the experience of riding on the back of a dragon.

Authentic Set Tour

The sets used in the filming of the TV show have been rebuilt at Westeros Northern Ireland. Walk through the same sets used by the actors and production crew of Game of Thrones. The same props and customes used by the actors are also here.

All Men (& Everyone Else) Must Dine

Hungry? Then head to any of the three restaurants and dine on a menu inspired by, and in some cases directly lifted from the show.
